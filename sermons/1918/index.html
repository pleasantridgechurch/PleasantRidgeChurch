1918 — Thanksgiving Gathering

Home | Sermon Archive | Attendance Ledger | Church History

Recorded: Late Autumn, 1918

Occasion: Thanksgiving Gathering

Notes

The gathering was held in abbreviated form. A reading of thanksgiving was offered. Attendance was not taken in the usual manner.

Certain household names appear in margin notation, but totals were not agreed upon. No full transcript of instruction was preserved.

The record for this gathering is considered sufficient.
