Pastoral Oversight

The church is guided by appointed ministers who oversee worship, teaching, and pastoral care. Sermons are delivered seasonally according to the needs of the congregation.

Elders

Elders are charged with maintaining doctrine, order, and discipline. They also oversee charitable distributions and resolve matters brought before the church.

Stewards

Stewards manage property, supplies, and records. Their work ensures continuity from generation to generation.

Community Roles

  • Record Keeper
  • Grounds Steward
  • Meal & Aid Coordinator
  • Bell Ringer (Seasonal)
