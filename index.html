Welcome. Pleasant Ridge Covenant Church is a small, faithful congregation committed to daily worship, vigilance, and stewardship of the land entrusted to us.

About the Archive

This site serves as a digital record of Pleasant Ridge Covenant Church, preserving selected sermons, attendance entries, and historical notes from across generations. Not all gatherings were documented fully, and not all records were intended to endure.

Materials are presented as they were received and retained according to their usefulness at the time. Absence of detail should not be taken as absence of practice.

Morning Worship is held daily at 9:00 AM.
